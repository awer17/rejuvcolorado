about Us

ReJuv Aesthetics is an advanced non surgical beauty clinic and luxury aesthetics based in Central Denver. With cutting edge technology and impeccable levels of customer service, we offer some of the most effective treatments on the market.

Our experienced professionals are extensively trained and qualified, specializing in Anti-Aging, Body Perfecting, Vein therapy, Rosacea, Acne, Sun damage,Scaring, Laser Hair Removal, Cutting edge anti-aging laser technologies, Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP), Neurotoxin Injections( Botox, Dysport), Dermal Fillers, Advanced Skin Solutions and Premium Beauty Treatments.

ReJuv Aesthetics continues to raise standards and has redefined the client experience aspiring for perfection without compromise.

I’m Ludmila Kling, a licensed aesthetician and the owner of Rejuv Aesthetics. As well as being a medical aesthetician I am also a laser specialist, certified injector, phlebotomist, and medical grade skin care expert. I'm a certified injector for PRP( Platelet-Rich Plasma), Botox, and dermal filler. I have extensive experience as a laser specialist with Sciton's adnanced technologies. I’m passionate about creating tailor made face and body beauty treatments to suit your needs and bring out the real beauty in you.

My goal is to provide you with an exceptional experience and great results in a peaceful and comfortable space.

A little about me: I love to learn and am always on the lookout for new cutting-edge technologies and engage in continuous trainings. I'm a family-oriented person and spend as much time as I can with them and our animals. I enjoy the outdoors and giving back to the community anyway I can. Including volunteering within my community, at women’s and children’s shelters, and at my son's school events.
